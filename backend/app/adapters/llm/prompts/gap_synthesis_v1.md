# Gap synthesis

From the field map below — and **only** from it, not from imagination —
enumerate the gaps in the literature for:

> {research_question}

Gap types to look for:
- `unanswered_question`: questions no analyzed paper answers;
- `untested_assumption`: assumptions shared across a cluster that no paper
  tests;
- `method_combination`: method pairings not yet tried;
- `unstudied_context`: populations / conditions / datasets not yet studied.

For each gap give `evidence` — the specific cluster/paper facts that reveal
it (cite `source_indexes`; add a short `passage` when a paper states the
boundary directly), an `importance` (high/medium/low) judged against the
research question, and an honest `confidence_label`.

Separately, propose `future_directions`: concrete suggestions for addressing
the most important gaps. These are your synthesis, not established fact —
they will be stored as speculative.
